About me

My favorite game right now is Heroes of the Storm where I am a savage in the Tank, Assassin, and Solo-Laner roles. I am trying to quit video games for the time being and focus on my career. If i can get as good at coding as I was at playing Heroes of the Storm, then I believe I could get any job I want. And the job I want is to become a Full-Stack developer.
I went to school for Mechanical engineering and I currently have an engineering job. While in school I was exposed to alot of engineering specific coding which I enjoyed a lot. I do like my current job, but my dream Is to use coding to solve all of my problems from engineering to business to robotics you name it. I know coding is an important part of life right now and will be a critical role in the future so I know I have to get in it now and learn all I can.

Taking this bootcamp will teach me how to keep learning everyday again. A lot of times you reach your goal like getting a bachelors degree and you think you are done learning. Being a developer, things are changing rapidly and new technologies are being created every day so you have to keep learning in this field. That is what attracts me to it.

I plan to can become a better coder, because right now as you can see I am not that good.
